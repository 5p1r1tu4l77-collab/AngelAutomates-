# Day 5 — Friday: Gmail burst + LinkedIn + book the calls

**Target touches today**: 50 (25 Gmail cold + 20 LinkedIn + Step-3 breakup on Wed cold emails + 1 FB group post #2)

This is the conversion day. If you've done Days 1–4 right, you should be sitting on 2–4 interested replies waiting to book. Block 1 closes them.

## Block 1 — Book the booked (90 min before lunch)

For every "interested" thread:
1. Confirm a slot in Cal.com.
2. Send a 1-paragraph pre-call brief: who you are, what to expect, how long it'll take, what they should think about beforehand.

### Pre-call brief template

```
Hey {{first_name}} —

We're set for {{day at time}}. To save us both time, here's what to expect:

- 12 minutes
- I'll ask 3 questions about your business
- I'll lay out what I do and the price
- You decide on the call (or after — no pressure)

Before the call, if you can have these numbers in your head it'll be way
more useful: avg ticket size, current monthly leads, monthly closes.

Talk Friday.

— {{your_first_name}}
```
