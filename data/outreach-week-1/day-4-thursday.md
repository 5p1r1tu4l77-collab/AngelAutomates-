## Block 1 — Reply handling (45 min)

By Day 4 you should have replies stacking up. Expected: 3–8 replies total since Monday.

For each reply, classify and respond:

| Class | Response |
|-------|----------|
| Interested | Send Cal.com link with proposed 2 times. Reply per `templates/cold-email.md` Step-2 style. |
| Question | Answer the question concretely. Reference `docs/OFFER.md`. Try to convert to a call. |
