# Offer (locked for first 90 days)

## What we sell

**20 qualified booked appointments per month — or your money back.**

We run a cold-email + reply-handling system for one of your top services. Every appointment that lands on your calendar is:

- Decision-maker (owner / GM / ops manager)
- Currently buying or expanding in our service category
- Confirmed in writing for a 30-min discovery call

## Who it's for

US home-services contractors with $500k–$10M annual revenue:

- Roofing
- HVAC
- Electrical
- Solar

Reason: ticket per closed job ($5k–$40k+) far exceeds our fee, ROI math closes the call.

## Price

- **$2,000 / month retainer**, 3-month minimum, monthly thereafter.
- OR **$150 / qualified appointment**, billed weekly, no minimum.

Most clients pick retainer (predictable cost).

## Guarantee

If we don't deliver 20 qualified appointments in any given month, the next month is free. After two missed months in a row, full refund of the most recent month.

## What you (the client) do

- 30-min weekly check-in
- Take the booked calls
- Tell us within 48h if any appointment was unqualified so we can replace it

## What we do (the agent fleet does)

- Source 1,500+ ICP-matched contacts/month
- Personalize every email with site research
- Send through warmed dedicated domains (never your domain)
- Triage replies, propose times, confirm bookings
- Weekly performance report

## Not included (upsell paths)

- Live phone answering / receptionist AI — $750/mo
- CRM integration (HubSpot, JobNimbus, ServiceTitan) — $2,500 one-time
- Local SEO + GBP optimization — $1,500/mo
- AI review-collection workflow — $1,500 one-time

## How we talk about it on a call

The discovery call has 3 jobs:
1. Confirm they have capacity to handle 20 new appointments/mo. If they're already maxed, propose Q2 start instead of refusing.
2. Confirm the math: avg ticket × close rate × 20 appts = expected revenue ÷ $2k = ROI multiple. Make them say the number.
3. Sign on the call when possible. DocuSign link sent before they hang up.
